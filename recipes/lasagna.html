Lasagna

Finished Lasagna Dish with Savory Taste and a Delicius Look

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and a step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat: the super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and ground beef;
  • Onion and garlic: onion and two cloves of garlic cooked with meat;
  • Tomato Produts: can of crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce and tomato paste;
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness;
  • Spices and seasonings: fresh parsley, basil leaves, salt and black pepper;
  • Lasagna noodes: home-made or store-bought;
  • Cheese: Parmsan, mozarella, and ricotta cheese for extra cheesiness;
  • Egg: egg helps bing together a ricotta.

How to Make Lasagna Step-by-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe ingredients
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How Long to Cook the Lasagna

The lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 220 degrees Celcius. Cover in with foil for the first 25minutes, then cook uncovered for the final 25 minues. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before cutting
